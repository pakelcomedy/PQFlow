How It Works

  1. 1. Scan or Click

    Use the QR code or provided link for your desired service.

  2. 2. Auto-Join

  3. 3. Track Progress

Real-Time Updates

Instant queue status powered by Firebase Firestore snapshots.

Offline Support

Continue to view your queue status even without an internet connection.

Multi-Language

Switch seamlessly between English and Bahasa Indonesia.

Secure & Private

All queue data AES-encrypted client-side before storage.

Auto-assign customers to the least-busy counter.

Priority Mode

Flag urgent cases to be served before regular queues.
