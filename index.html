How It Works

  1. 1. Scan or Tap

    Scan the QR code or open a provided link to instantly join the queue for a specific service or counter.

  2. 2. Get Your Number

    Your ticket number is generated automatically — no form or registration required.

  3. 3. Wait for Your Turn

    Your number will be displayed when it’s called. Track queue progress directly on your device.

Key Features

Real-Time Updates

Live ticket calls and position tracking powered by Firebase.

Multiple Counters

Tickets can be served by any available counter in your organization.

Simple Admin

Admins can call, skip, or reset tickets from a web dashboard.
